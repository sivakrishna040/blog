Biryani Recipe

Biryani is a classic Indian dish made with fragrant rice layered with spiced meat or vegetables. Here's a simple recipe for Chicken Biryani:

  1. Marinate chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, chili powder, and salt.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add whole spices (cinnamon, cardamom, cloves), and saut√© onions until golden.
  3. Add marinated chicken and cook until it's almost done.
  4. Layer cooked rice over the chicken, sprinkle saffron-infused milk, fried onions, and chopped mint-coriander leaves.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat until the flavors blend and the rice is fully cooked.
  6. Serve hot with raita (yogurt dip) and salad.
